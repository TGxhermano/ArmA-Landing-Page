ABOUT US

Who we are

TG Arma Division is a mature group of people across the world who enjoy to play the game with other like-minded players. Arma Division was created on the 26th of June 2013 as a subdivision of the TacticalGaming.net Community and has been going strong ever since.

We consist of two Battalions: 1st Battalion, based in North America and 2nd Battalion, based in Europe with own servers & schedules. We also have our Reserve Forces which consists of members from other TG game divisions.

How we play

Arma 3 is a military simulation with an emphasis on realism. That's why we focus on the tactical aspect of the game, using real-life military tactics, structures, equipment and communication in a combined arms environment. However we are not a hardcore milsim group who go full-blown-drill-sergeant on any other occasion. Our primary goal is to have a realistic gameplay while enjoying our time.

We play organized and well-written (OPORD/Briefing) Zeus Co-OP missions on Wednesdays and Sundays, mixing it up with training drills, optional missions & occasional TvT/PvP events. We have a set of ever-growing SOP's (Standard Operating Procedures) as a tactical standard reference with a certification system, which enables members to use special assets properly. We have no fixed role system and encourage everybody to master every aspect of the game.

The training can consist of anything from small-unit-tactics, recon-, flight-, airborne jump training, map reading, mission planning and leading to sniper school, JTAC, mortar and CQB/MOUT. You will have intense and immersive gameplay, all in our family-like group of Arma enthusiasts.

However we understand that people have work, school and family life outside the community. It's important for us to keep our members within Tactical Gaming and hence we have a real-life-first approach. We're proud to say that most of our veterans are long time members - the core of the division that became good friends. People, who go on leave for some time, come back to us. It shows the strength and comradery that Tactical Gaming Arma Division brings to the table.

What we offer

We...
  • have a mature, dedicated but always real-life-first mindset
  • US and EU battalions with own servers and practice times
  • a focus on demanding, tactical COOP Zeus missions
  • plus optional missions during the week
  • plus occasional PvP / TvT events
  • DLCs: Laws of War ++
  • Mod base: CBA, ACE, TFAR, RHS, MCC, CUP...
  • use Teamspeak 3 & Discord

What you bring

You...
  • are older than 18 years
  • have a working microphone / headphones
  • ability to speak English
  • a mature mindset focusing on tactics & team progression rather than your own killscore
  • ability to attend at least one of the practices each week
